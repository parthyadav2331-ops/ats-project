💡 Tips to Maximize Your ATS Score

Use keywords directly from the job description in your resume.
Avoid tables, graphics, and columns — ATS systems often can't read them.
Use standard section headings like "Experience", "Education", and "Skills".
Submit your resume as a PDF unless the job posting asks for a Word file.
Quantify achievements — numbers stand out to both ATS and human reviewers.
